Answers

Common questions about working with FrameFlow.

How long does a typical project take?
+

It depends on the scope. A logo takes 1–2 weeks, a full brand identity 2–4 weeks, and a website 4–8 weeks. We'll give you a detailed timeline during your free consultation.

Do you work with businesses outside of Toronto?
+

Absolutely. While we're based in Toronto, we work with clients across Canada and internationally. All our processes are designed to work seamlessly remotely.

What does the free consultation involve?
+

It's a 30-minute call where we listen to your goals, understand your business, and recommend the best path forward — no pressure, no sales pitch. Just honest, strategic advice.

How does the client portal work?
+

Once you're onboarded, you get access to your private client portal where you can view your social media schedule, review and approve post designs, give feedback, and communicate directly with our team — all in one place.

What are your payment terms?
+

We typically require 50% upfront to begin work, with the remaining 50% due on delivery. For ongoing retainer services, we invoice monthly at the start of each period.
